\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
At each level in the stack where the exception is caught and rethrown a new \doxylink{classfc_1_1log__message}{log\+\_\+message} is added to the exception.

exception\textquotesingle{}s are designed to be serialized to a variant and deserialized from an variant.
