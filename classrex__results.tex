\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The actions {\ttfamily buyresult}, {\ttfamily sellresult}, {\ttfamily rentresult}, and {\ttfamily orderresult} of {\ttfamily rex.\+results} are all no-\/ops. They are added as inline convenience actions to {\ttfamily rentnet}, {\ttfamily rentcpu}, {\ttfamily buyrex}, {\ttfamily unstaketorex}, and {\ttfamily sellrex}. An inline convenience action does not have any effect, however, its data includes the result of the parent action and appears in its trace.
