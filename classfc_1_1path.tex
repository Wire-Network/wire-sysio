\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Most calls are simply a passthrough to boost\+::filesystem\+::path, however exceptions are wrapped in an fc\+::error\+\_\+report and the additional helper method \doxylink{classfc_1_1path_a12a75eca015d0c9880e2c062e371d08d}{fc\+::path\+::windows\+\_\+string()}, can be used to calculate paths intended for systems different than the host.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
Serializes to a fc\+::value() as the result of \doxylink{classfc_1_1path_ab1eae2062df4ea01435348ec52fb3784}{generic\+\_\+string()} 
\end{DoxyNote}
